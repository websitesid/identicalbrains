ABOUT
IDENTICAL
BRAINS

info@identicalbrains.com

Creators have stories to tell and we have tools to bring their stories to life. It’s the translation of imagination from ideas to words to visuals. Identical Brains is backed with a strong workforce that will manifest your imagination. Right after a script has been locked, our artists will help you identify every possible avenue within the narrative to bring your story powerfully alive. VFX is one of the most powerful tools of filmmaking and yet an underdog. We believe every idea is a strong one, and it’s the approach towards execution that makes a difference. Hence our message to the creators is think VFX, Imagine Reality.

OUR TOOLBOX

Visual Effects
A process by which visuals - graphics - images are created or manipulated to create live-action shots. It’s a tool to create realistic imagery, popularly known as VFX.
Online & Offline Editing

Editing is one of the most crucial processes of filmmaking. Editor’s cut, director’s cut a story can be told in multiple styles, and specialising in edits is what make IB so special.

Sound Designing and Mixing

In a world where the audience is tuning to ASMR videos, a lot more emphasis needs to be given to sound designing and mixing. We will provide you with the most updated sound mixing tech.

Colour Grading

Process of improving the appearance of an image for presentation by using various attributes like contrast, saturation, detail for motion pictures or video
